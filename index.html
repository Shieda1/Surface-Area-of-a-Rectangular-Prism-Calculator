How to calculate surface area of rectangular prism?
To calculate surface area of rectangular prism you should use the following formula:
surface area of rectangular prism = 2 * (width of the rectangular prism * length of the rectangular prism + length of the rectangular prism * height of the rectangular prism + height of the rectangular prism * width of the rectangular prism)
